Simon Vary

I am a fourth year DPhil student in mathematics at University of Oxford working with Prof. Jared Tanner in the Numerical Analysis Group.

I work on low-rank plus sparse matrix models, compressed sensing, and matrix/tensor completion, with applications in multispectral imaging and remote sensing.

My doctoral dissertation was on low-rank plus sparse matrix sensing.
