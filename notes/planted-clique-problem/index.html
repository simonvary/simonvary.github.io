Planted clique problem

Nov 3, 2021

The following is very difficult to solve

$$ \max_{x} \lVert A x \rVert_2, \quad \mathrm{s.t.} \quad \lVert x\rVert_0 \leq s, \lVert x\rVert_2 = 1, $$

when $s = \mathcal{O}(\log(n))$ and reasonably difficult when $s = \mathcal{O}(\sqrt{n})$
